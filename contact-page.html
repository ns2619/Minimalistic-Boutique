Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or directly with the shipping carrier.
What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging. Custom or personalized items cannot be returned unless they are damaged or defective.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship to select international destinations. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by country. Please note that customs fees and import duties may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.
How do I care for my handmade ceramic items?
Most of our ceramic items are dishwasher and microwave safe, but we recommend hand washing for longevity. Avoid sudden temperature changes and harsh cleaning chemicals. Specific care instructions are included with each product.
